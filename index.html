Molten Core

Dispel curses and magic debuffs. Pull with hunters. Kill adds first.

Fear rotation required. Use tranquilizing shot to manage frenzy.

Sample Loot

  • Choker of Enlightenment – Great for casters
  • Obsidian Edged Blade – Solid for Warriors and Paladins
  • Bindings of the Windseeker – Legendary quest item
