Overview

Molten Core is the first 40-man raid in Classic World of Warcraft, located deep within Blackrock Mountain. Expect fire damage, add-heavy fights, and lots of trash.

Trash Mobs

  • Molten Giants: Knockback cleave. Keep them faced away from the raid.
  • Lava Surger: Charges ranged targets. High threat. Needs quick taunts.
  • Firelord: Summons Flame Imps. AOE them down quickly.

Lucifron

Dispel curses and magic debuffs. Pull with hunters. Kill adds first.

Magmadar

Fear rotation required. Use tranquilizing shot to manage frenzy.

Loot Summary

Molten Core drops Tier 1 set pieces and many fire-themed weapons. See the Loot page for full breakdowns and priority suggestions.
